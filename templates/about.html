About Us

Our Mission

The Satellite Image Processor is a tool designed to help researchers and enthusiasts normalize satellite imagery for consistent analysis. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform for processing images with precision and efficiency.

Our Team

We are a team of developers and data scientists passionate about space exploration and image processing. Contact us at info@satelliteprocessor.com.
